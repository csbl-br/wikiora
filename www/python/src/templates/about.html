About WikiORA

WikiORA is a helper for Over-Representation Analysis of gene sets based on content available on Wikidata. The tool is available for two species: mouse (Mus musculus) and human (Homo sapiens). For each species, there are two sources of markers in the platform: genes linked to Gene Ontology biological processes and genes linked to cell type markers (mostly from PanglaoDB).

A hypergeometric test is run against the database to calculate overly represented gene sets. Genes in the list may be separated by tabs, semicolons, commas, or new lines.

Methods
